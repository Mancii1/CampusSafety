Safety Tips and Guidelines for Campus Life

Here are some important safety tips and guidelines to follow while on campus:

  • Always be aware of your surroundings.
  • Report any suspicious activity to campus security.
  • Keep your personal belongings secure.
  • Use well-lit paths and walkways at night.
  • Do not prop open exterior doors.
  • Do not leave valuables unattended.
  • Always lock your dorm or apartment door.
  • Do not walk alone at night.
  • Trust your instincts.
